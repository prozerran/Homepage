Something About Me

Hello there! My name is Tim Hsu Yee Feng. Yes, you can call me Tim, but alot of people know me by my Chinese name Hsu Yee Feng or 徐宇鋒. Anyways, please to meet you.

A little something about myself. I was born in China, but grew up in the US since I was a kid. There, I took education and went to San Francisco State University and graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. Ever since then, I became a geeky programmer. Ouch! Oh well, that's life, you got to choose a profession that you like, right?

Yes, I took up some programming jobs in the US, but although I was generally very successful in the States, I wasn't very happy there. So I moved to a better city, in Hong Kong! Yes, I know the salary there is substantially lower then that of the States, but I love being in a city that is full of life, where i can safely go out at midnight and still be able to get some fishball at the street market. You can't do that in the US.

Anyways, I might create a blog about my adventures one day, but I don't have that kind of time now. But for now, if you wish to know me more, we can add each other as friends. I have a Facebook account here.
